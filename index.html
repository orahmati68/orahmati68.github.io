Info

Greetings, welcome to my home page. My research focus is on the application of geospatial techniques, soft computing, and GIS-based machine learning models to problem-solving in natural resources and environmental management with emphasis on sustainable development. Our papers appear in a wide variety of different scientific journals, and describe methodological advances, and their application to understand main processes in geomorphology, hydrogeology, geostatistics, surface hydrology, and water resources. The state-of-the-art models and artificial intelligent techniques allow us to detect the relationship between geo-environmental variables and manage problematic issues (e.g., land use change, erosion, etc.).
